## Style Notes for Rathergood Aesthetic

The worms should look:
- **Simple** - Not over-detailed
- **Slightly crude** - Intentional jank
- **Expressive** - Big reactions
- **Pink/flesh-toned** - Classic earthworm color

Key features to add:
- **Dot eyes** (or simple circle eyes)
- **Simple mouth** that can open wide
- **Segmented body** (optional - could be smooth tube)
- **No legs** (just a tube with a face)

The free assets above are starting points - you'll customize them to match the rathergood vibe!
